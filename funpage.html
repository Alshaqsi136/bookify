How to Play
  1. Read the target sentence shown at the top
  2. Click words from the available words section to add them to your sentence
  3. Words will appear in the "Your Sentence" area in the order you click them
  4. Click the "X" button on a word to remove it from your sentence
  5. Click "Check Answer" when you think your sentence is correct
  6. Earn points for correct sentences and advance to the next level!
